Chocolate Chip Cookies

Recipe 4: Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup Butter, softened
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Cup Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp Salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Add egg, vanilla, and mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture, then fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool before serving.
