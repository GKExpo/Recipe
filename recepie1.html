Spaghetti Carbonara

Recipe 1: Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl.
  4. Combine the pasta, pancetta, and egg mixture. Toss well.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.
